### SEO Requirements

#### Technical SEO
- **Readability Score**: 8-9/10 target
- **Paragraph Length**: Maximum 40-80 words
- **Sentence Length**: Average 15-20 words
- **Internal Links**: 3-5 relevant links per article
- **Schema Markup**: Complete JSON-LD implementation (Organization, Article, Person, FAQPage)

#### Content Quality
- **Word Count**: Minimum 1500 words for comprehensive guides
- **Originality**: 100% unique content with original research
- **Accuracy**: All facts verified with professional validation
- **Freshness**: Current 2025 information and requirements

### Writing Style and Tone

#### Voice and Approach
- **Tone**: Professional but accessible, conversational yet authoritative
- **Language**: Clear and concise, avoid unnecessary technical jargon
- **Voice**: Active and engaging, use "you" to connect with readers
- **Structure**: Logical flow with natural transitions between sections

- Use varied structure: narrative → checklist → table → quotes

- Use comparative tables for decision-making
- Include interactive-style checklists
- Add timeline visualizations for processes
